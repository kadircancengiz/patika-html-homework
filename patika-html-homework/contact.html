Contacting Apple

Sales and Product Inquiries

Apple Online Store
Apple.com is a convenient place to purchase Apple products and accessories from Apple and other
manufacturers. You can buy online or call (800) MY-APPLE (800-692-7753).

You can get information about an order you placed on the Apple Online Store through the Order Status page.
If you prefer, you can also get order status or make changes by phone at (800-692-7753).
